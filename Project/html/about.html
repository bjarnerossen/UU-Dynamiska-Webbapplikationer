About

Our Story

Grocery Guru was founded in 2023 by a food enthusiast named Bjarne who wanted to make grocery shopping easier for everyone. He noticed that many people struggle with meal planning and grocery shopping, so he decided to create a platform that would help them save time, money, and effort.

Our goal is to provide you with the best grocery shopping experience possible. We believe that by using our platform, you can become a grocery guru too!
